A Painter's Handy Guide to Mixing KEIM Concretal-Lasur

The 2 Key Components

First, let's get our heads around the products you'll be working with:

  • KEIM Concretal-Lasur (The "Pigment"): This is the colour part of the mix. You can also use it on its own for a solid (opaque) finish.
  • KEIM Concretal-Fixativ (The "Dilution"): This is the clear fixer. You mix this with the colour to make it more transparent (like a stain).

The Golden Rule

You can mix the Pigment (colour) and the Dilution (clear fixer) in ANY RATIO you want. This is not like a 2-part epoxy; there is no "correct" chemical mix. The ratio you choose is an artistic choice to get the transparency you want.

IMPORTANT WARNING: THE TEST PATCH IS ESSENTIAL!

Concrete walls and ceilings naturally vary in colour and texture. A key reason to use Lasur is to even out these differences.

Your test patch is where you perfect your mix. Apply a small amount in an inconspicuous area and let it dry completely. You will likely need to adjust your ratio (add more Pigment or Dilution) and re-test to achieve your desired finish.

The final look is heavily influenced by the concrete's porosity, its existing base colour, and its exposure to light. What works on one wall may look slightly different on another.

How to Use The Calculator

Example 1: Mixing by Ratio

Goal: Mix 1 Litre (1000ml) with a transparent 1 : 4 ratio.

  1. In "Total Volume", type 1000.
  2. Select the "By Ratio" tab.
  3. Enter 1 for Pigment and 4 for Dilution.

The calculator will show:

  • Pigment: 200 ml
  • Dilution: 800 ml

Example 2: Mixing by Percentage

Goal: Mix 1 Litre (1000ml) with 30% Pigment for a transparent finish.

  1. In "Total Volume", type 1000.
  2. Select the "By Percentage" tab.
  3. Enter 30 in the percentage box.

The calculator will show:

  • Pigment: 300 ml
  • Dilution: 700 ml

How to Mix (Step-by-Step)

  1. Prepare your gear: Use a clean, dry mixing bucket. A mechanical mixer (like a drill attachment) is highly recommended for this.
  2. Stir the Pigment First: Thoroughly mix the Concretal-Lasur (Pigment) in its original can before pouring. The colour settles at the bottom!
  3. Combine: Pour your measured amounts of Pigment and Dilution (Fixativ) into your bucket.
  4. Mix Well: Mix thoroughly with your mechanical mixer for at least 2 minutes.
  5. Keep stirring: Keep stirring the mix every 10-15 minutes while you work. The colour can settle again quickly.

Crucial Application Rules

  • DO NOT DILUTE WITH WATER.

    This will ruin the chemical bond of the silicate paint. Only use Concretal-Fixativ to dilute.

  • Work "Wet-on-Wet".

    Always keep a "wet edge" as you paint. Do not stop in the middle of a wall, or you will see a line. Paint from one corner to another, or from one joint to another, without stopping.

  • Apply thinly.

    This is a stain, not a thick paint. Apply thin, even coats.
